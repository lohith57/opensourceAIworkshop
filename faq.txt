Electronics Store FAQ

1. Warranty Registration:
To register your product for warranty, visit our website and go to the "My Account" section. Select "Warranty Registration" and enter your order number and product details. You'll receive a confirmation email once registered. Warranty periods vary by product, typically 1-2 years.

2. Return Policy:
We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be in original condition with packaging. To initiate a return, log in to your account, go to "My Orders," select the order, and click "Request Return." We'll provide a return label. Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after receipt.

3. Order Cancellation:
You can cancel an order within 24 hours of placement if it hasn't shipped. Go to "My Orders" and select "Cancel Order." If the order has shipped, follow the return policy instead. Cancellations after 24 hours may incur fees.

4. Delivery Timeline:
Standard delivery takes 3-7 business days depending on your location. Express shipping is available for an additional fee, delivering in 1-3 business days. You'll receive tracking information via email once shipped.

5. Product Support:
For technical support, contact our support team at support@electronicsstore.com or call 1-800-123-4567. Provide your product model and issue details. We also have online resources and manuals available on product pages.

6. Payment Help:
We accept credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. If you encounter payment issues, ensure your billing information matches your card details. For further assistance, contact payments@electronicsstore.com.
